DEER

Scientific Name: Cervidae

Habitat: Found in forests, grasslands, and hilly areas around the world.

Appearance: Deer have slender bodies, long legs, and a short tail. Males usually have antlers.

Diet: Herbivores that eat grass, leaves, fruits, and twigs.

Behavior: Deer are shy and gentle animals that usually move in groups called herds.

Adaptability: They can adapt to different climates and environments.

Speed: Deer can run very fast to escape predators.

Importance: Deer help in seed dispersal and maintaining the ecosystem balance.

Symbolism: Deer symbolize gentleness, grace, and peace.
